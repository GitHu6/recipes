1 pound of skinless chicken breast cut into cubes
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 cup sliced yellow onion
1 cup shiitake mushrooms
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 table spoon Gochujang (Korean Chili Paste)
2 tablespoons canola oil

whisk half cup oil and Gochujang and set aside 

Set stove top to medium heat, add cooking oil, chicken cubes and cook till 
half done then add minced garlic and onions and cook covered till onions
are slightly translucent.  Add mushrooms, salt and pepper and stir then add
pour soy sauce mixture and stir then cover and allow to cook for a few minutes. 
Uncover turn heat to maximum and keep stirring until sauce caramelizes.  Then
turn the stove off.


Serve on top of steam jasmine rice and a fried egg (sunny side or over easy)
